<p>The Character &amp; Leadership Development Department (CLD) provides a
list of resources for educators, coaches and sport providers who are interested
in character and leadership development through sport for the youth sector
and team development through sport for the corporate sector.</p>
<h3><strong>Character and Leadership Development Through Sport</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Game For Life - Lives Made Extraordinary Through Sport</strong></h4>
<p>The book, 'GAME FOR LIFE: 25 Journeys' chronicles the transformation of
25 ordinary lives made extraordinary through sports. Featuring 25 individuals
from diverse backgrounds playing different sports, it aims to inspire readers
to play the game of their lives, and in so doing, discover themselves,
unleash their true potential and develop their character.</p>
<p>'GAME FOR LIFE: 25 Journeys' celebrates the triumph of man, showcasing
role models with a spirit of excellence and perseverance. The 25 remarkable
stories are set to stir the hearts and minds of every reader, young or
old, man or woman. Instilling in all the principles of team camaraderie,
sportsmanship, leadership and sporting for life.</p>
<p>The ebook is now available for <a href="/files/Sport%20Education/Sports%20Leadership/Resource/Game_For_Life_Book20130515.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">download</a>.</p>

<h4><strong>Game for Life: Transforming Lives through Sport Documentary Series</strong></h4>
<p>Featuring an all-Singaporean cast of coaches, this landmark series by
CoachSG enters into the Heart of Coaching. With a common thread of building
team culture, fostering a conducive environment for holistic development
and having a strong purpose, these inspiring coaches detail their personal
coaching values and beliefs. These guide their inner compass as they transform
lives through Sport.</p>
<p>Seven coaches from across sports are featured in this series, Swimming
(Ang Peng Siong), Athletics (C. Kunalan), Football (Loh Ngiap Tai), Floorball
(Louise Khng), Volleyball (Teo Siew Lan) and Unified Football (Ardi Ramli
&amp; Lionel Teo).</p>
<p>Find out more about the Full Series here<a href="https://coachsg.sportsingapore.gov.sg/game-for-life-series" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<h4><strong>CoachSG Partners Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA)</strong></h4>
<p>CoachSG partners&nbsp;<a href="https://www.positivecoach.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA)</a>&nbsp;to
offer positive, character-building youth sports coaching resources to help
coaches and sport educators develop teams to be Game For Life. These resources
include online articles and courses. PCA’s goal is to develop better athletes,
better people and transform the youth sports culture into a Development
Zone&nbsp;®&nbsp;where all youth and high school athletes have a positive,
character-building experience. Visit their&nbsp;<a href="https://www.positivecoach.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">website</a>&nbsp;today for coaching
resources.</p>
