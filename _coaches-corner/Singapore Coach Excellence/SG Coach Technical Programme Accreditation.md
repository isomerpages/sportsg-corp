<p>SG-Coach Technical Programme covers sport-specific considerations (such
as sport demands, specific training methodologies, techniques, and sport
strategies) while SG-Coach Integrated Programme incorporates both Theory
component and sport-specific considerations. Hence, possession of an Integrated
certificate would be viewed as possession of both Theory and Technical
certifications. Both Technical and Integrated Programme are delivered via
a mixture of classroom-based and workshop-based teaching with assessments
that may come in the form of assignment, demonstration, and / or examination.</p>
<p>There are two methods of accreditation available for interested individuals:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Accreditation through Coursework: This is suitable for individuals who
wish to gain knowledge and / or competencies through attending classes,
workshops and assessment. Please contact the respectively National Sports
Association (NSA) (refer to list below) for course information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Accreditation through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL): This is suitable
for individuals who had formal learning in areas of coaching. All Technical
/ Integrated accreditation via RPL are subjected to the evaluation and
approval of the relevant NSA. Please contact the respective NSA (refer
to list below) for more information:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The following table shows a list of sports with SG-Coach coaching courses,
where:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Level 1 Full Integration – comprises Foundational Sports Science (FSS),
SG-Coach Level 1 Theory and SG-Coach Level 1 Technical components</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Level 1 Integrated – comprises SG-Coach L1 Theory and SG-Coach L1 Technical
components only</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Level 1 Technical – comprises SG-Coach L1 Technical component only</p>
</li>
</ul>
